About Me


I am a Bristol based stand-up comedian. I'm a previous winner of the Chortle Student Comedian of the Year, Comedy Gladiators, and a finalist in the Laughing Horse New Act of the Year.


I'm also a researcher at the University of Bristol in the area of protein design. I'm interested in science communication and am doing a show about synthetic biology at the Edinburgh Festival Fringe.
