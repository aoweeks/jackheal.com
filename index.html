About Me


I work at the University of Bristol, researching protein design. This is one of many topics in the field of synthetic biology.


As well as science, I enjoy performing stand-up comedy. I'm a previous winner of the Chortle Student Comedian of the Year.



I'm particularly interested in science communication: talking about science and making it funny at the same time.
